Greek Traditional Adult Choir of the Church of Pantovasilissa

At the heart of Rafina stands the Church of Panagia Pantovasilissa, built in the mid-20th century by refugees from Triglia, Minor Asia, as a living memorial to their origins. The choir meets here in a welcoming spirit, keeping alive the richness of Greek traditional music.

Rehearsals, conducted in Greek, bring together traditional songs from all corners of Greece, nurturing an authentic style and soulful storytelling. Expect a space that's welcoming, culturally rich, and alive.

✓Traditional repertoire from all over Greece
✓ Vocal guidance in authentic folk style
✓Participation in community events and performances

Greek Traditional Choir of Roumeliotes

The Roumeliotes Association of Northeastern Attica, based in Nea Makri, was founded to preserve and share the rich cultural heritage of Central Greece. Within this spirit, our choir gives special emphasis to the folk songs of Roumeli, while also embracing musical traditions from all over Greece.

Rehearsals are friendly and engaging, offering both musical learning and community bonding. The choir often participates in cultural celebrations and performances, sharing the vibrant sound of Greek folk song with wider audiences.

✓Folk song traditions of Roumeli and wider Greece
✓Opportunities for performances and festivals
✓Connection with cultural preservation and community
✓Vocal guidance in authentic folk style

Association of Friends of Music, Rafina

The Association of Friends of Music in Rafina offers a welcoming space for music lovers of all ages. Our programs focus on Byzantine music, Greek traditional singing, and choral activities, fostering creativity, musical skill, and community connection. Classes are designed for children, adults, and seniors alike.
