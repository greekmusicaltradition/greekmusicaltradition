Local Greek Traditional & Byzantine Music Lessons

Experience Greek Music in Our Local Community

Immerse yourself in the rich tradition of Greek music through in-person lessons and ensemble participation!

Our local lessons offer the irreplaceable experience of learning Greek traditional and Byzantine music in person, surrounded by a community of fellow musicians. Feel the resonance of authentic instruments, experience the power of group singing, and connect with centuries-old traditions in an intimate, supportive environment.

Individual local lessons provide personalized, hands-on instruction where you can experience the full acoustic richness of traditional Greek music and receive immediate, tactile feedback on your technique.

Group lessons and choir participation offer the authentic communal experience of Greek music-making, where you'll learn to blend your voice with others and experience the powerful energy of ensemble singing.
