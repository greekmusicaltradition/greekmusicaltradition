Greek Traditional Adult Choir of the Church of Pantovasilissa

At the heart of Rafina stands the Church of Panagia Pantovasilissa, built in the mid-20th century by refugees from Triglia, Minor Asia, as a living memorial to their origins. The choir meets here in a welcoming spirit, keeping alive the richness of Greek traditional music.

Rehearsals, conducted in Greek, bring together traditional songs from all corners of Greece, nurturing an authentic style and soulful storytelling. Expect a space that's welcoming, culturally rich, and alive with song.

Greek Traditional Choir of Roumeliotes

The Roumeliotes Association of Northeastern Attica, rooted in Nea Makri, was founded to preserve and share the rich cultural heritage of Central Greece. Within this spirit, our choir gives special emphasis to the folk songs of Roumeli, while also embracing musical traditions from all over Greece.

Rehearsals are friendly and engaging, offering both musical learning and community bonding. The choir often participates in cultural celebrations and performances, sharing the vibrant sound of Greek folk song with wider audiences.

McGeorge Conservatory, Rafina

The McGeorge Conservatory is a modern cultural landmark in Rafina, known for its high-quality music education and focus on the classical tradition. Today, it also embraces the richness of Greece's own musical heritage, with programs in Byzantine music and Greek traditional singing, now officially established as part of conservatory studies in Greece.
