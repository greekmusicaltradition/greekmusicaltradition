Frequently Asked Questions

What types of lessons do you offer?
We offer both Byzantine music lessons and Greek traditional singing lessons. Our lessons cover repertoire, music theory, notation, and vocal technique.
Are lessons available online?
Yes! We offer both one-to-one and group online lessons, making our programs accessible to students worldwide.
Do I need prior musical experience?
Not at all! We welcome students of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced musicians looking to specialize in Greek musical traditions.
In what languages are lessons taught?
Lessons are available in both English and Greek, depending on your preference and comfort level.
How do I get started?
Simply fill out the contact form above with your interests and experience level, and we'll get back to you to discuss your musical goals and schedule a first lesson.
