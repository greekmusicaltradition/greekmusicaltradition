Greek Traditional & Byzantine Music

Learn Authentic Greek Music

If you're drawn to the beauty and depth of Greek traditional and Byzantine music, you're in the right place!

I offer lessons that deepen your connection to this rich and living tradition while strengthening and training your voice. You'll be supported in developing your vocal skills while gaining a deeper understanding of the unique character of each musical style.

In one-to-one lessons, I provide step-by-step guidance tailored to your voice, goals, and pace, helping you build vocal strength, flexibility, and confidence.

Group lessons, on the other hand, offer a unique opportunity to learn in a shared musical environment that encourages listening, interaction, and a sense of community.

Getting Started & Requirements

How to Get Started

  1. Contact Us:
    Reach out through our contact form to discuss your interests and level.
  2. Schedule a trial lesson:
    A discounted price is offered to all new students for the first 4 lessons.
  3. Choose Your Format:
    Select between one-to-one-one or group lessons.
  4. Book one-to-one lesson:
    You can book an individual lesson, according to availability, using the scheduler.
  5. Join our waiting list for group lessons:
    Groups run at specific times. Join our waiting list to be informed when a new group lesson launches.
  6. Book recurring lessons:
    Check the calendar for availability and then contact me to manually book the same date and time, weekly, with a monthly subscription
