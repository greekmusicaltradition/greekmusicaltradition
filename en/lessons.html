Greek Traditional & Byzantine Music

Learn Authentic Greek Music

Our comprehensive music lessons offer you the opportunity to learn the authentic sounds and techniques of Greek traditional and Byzantine music.

Lessons are conducted online, offering you the flexibility to learn from anywhere in the world, but also locally, if you live nearby or are visiting.

Choose between one-to-one personalized instruction or subscribe to be informed about the group lessons when they are available.

Lessons are available in both English and Greek to serve our diverse community of learners.
