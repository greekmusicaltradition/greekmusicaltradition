My Musical Journey

My musical journey began at the **Experimental Music High School of Pallini**, but it wasn't until 2012, through traditional Greek dance, that I reconnected deeply with music. That experience led me to join traditional music choirs, dive into study once again, and follow a path that brought me to the heart of this living tradition.

I hold a **Diploma in Byzantine Music** from the Music School of the Holy Metropolis of Mesogaia and Lavreotiki (Rafina branch), and a **Degree in Greek Traditional Singing** from the **Protoporia Conservatory**, where I studied under the guidance of renowned musician **Christos Tsiaboulis**.

These formal studies, combined with years of practical experience, have given me a deep appreciation for both the sacred and folk traditions that form the backbone of Greek musical culture.

My Approach

My teaching style is personal, respectful, and rooted in tradition. I believe that every student brings their own unique voice and story to these ancient melodies. Whether you're reconnecting with your Greek heritage, exploring these traditions for the first time, or seeking to deepen your existing knowledge, I create a supportive environment where you can grow at your own pace.

Each lesson is tailored to your individual needs, focusing not just on technique, but on the emotional and cultural context that makes this music so powerful. I help students understand the stories behind the songs, the proper pronunciation of Greek lyrics, and the subtle ornamentations that bring these melodies to life.

Whether you're just beginning or seeking to go deeper, I would be honored to walk with you on your musical journey.
