About Byzantine Music

Rooted in the liturgy of the Orthodox Church, Byzantine music weaves spirituality and history through unique modal scales, ornate chant, and timeless melodies. It represents one of the world's oldest continuous musical traditions, dating back over 1,500 years.

From the haunting melodies of ancient ballads to the passionate calls of work songs, Greek traditional singing carries the emotional essence of generations through every note.
