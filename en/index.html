Greek Traditional & Byzantine Music

Discover the Music Tradition of Greece

Welcome! I am Olga, Founder of the greekmusicaltradition.com website.

I'm delighted that you're here! Together we can explore a world of beautiful melodies and timeless traditions, where the music of Greece comes alive.

Whether you are looking for one-on-one mentorship with me or joining our community, I invite you to explore the rich musical heritage of Greece through authentic lessons in Greek traditional singing and Byzantine music. We'll journey through centuries of musical artistry, from ancient Byzantine chant to vibrant folk melodies, guided by a deep respect for this living tradition.

Lessons are available in both English and Greek and conducted online, offering you the flexibility to learn from anywhere in the world, but also locally, if you live nearby or are visiting the area.

Let's begin this melodic journey together.
