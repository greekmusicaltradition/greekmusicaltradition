Online Lessons

Connect with the beauty and depth of Greek traditional singing and Byzantine music through personalized online instruction!

My online lessons bring the authentic Greek musical tradition directly to you, wherever you are in the world. You'll receive the same quality instruction and personal attention as in-person lessons, with the added convenience of learning from your own space.

In one-to-one online lessons, I provide step-by-step guidance tailored to your voice, goals, and pace through high-quality video sessions, helping you build vocal strength, flexibility, and confidence.

Online group lessons offer a unique opportunity to learn in a shared virtual musical environment that encourages listening, interaction, and connection with fellow students worldwide.

Technical Requirements

  • Stable high-speed internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone with HD camera
  • Good quality microphone or headset for clear audio
  • Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet installed
  • Quiet learning environment free from interruptions
  • PDF reader for digital sheet music

Digital Materials Provided

  • High-quality digital sheet music and notation
  • Audio recordings and practice tracks
  • Video demonstrations and technique examples
  • Cultural and historical context materials
  • Practice exercises and personalized assignments
  • Lesson recordings for review (upon request)
