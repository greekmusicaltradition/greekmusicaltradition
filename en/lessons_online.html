Technical Requirements

  • Stable high-speed internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone with HD camera
  • Good quality microphone or headset for clear audio
  • Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet installed
  • Quiet learning environment free from interruptions
  • PDF reader for digital sheet music

Digital Materials Provided

  • High-quality digital sheet music and notation
  • Audio recordings and practice tracks
  • Video demonstrations and technique examples
  • Cultural and historical context materials
  • Practice exercises and personalized assignments
  • Lesson recordings for review (upon request)

How to Get Started with Online Lessons

  1. Schedule Your first two lessons: Discounted rates for your first two trial lessons
    Contact us and we will guide you through the process and offer you a special discount, only available for the first two lessons and by contacting us.
  2. Choose Your Learning Path: Select individual or group online lessons
    Decide between personalized one-to-one instruction or join our online group sessions to learn alongside students from around the world.
  3. Schedule Your on-to-one Online Lesson: Visit our lesson link to get to our calendar
    Have a look at the available time slots and order when you find one that suits you. If you are unable to find a convenient time, Contact us and we will try our best to find a suitable time slot for you.
  4. Regular Online Sessions: Establish a consistent online learning routine offered at a discounted price
    Contact us to set up weekly recurring online lessons at a time that works for your schedule.
